Haitian pattie

How to make a yummy haitian pattie

A pattie in itself is any item of food covered with dough or batter and fried or baked.

The only thing that makes a Haitian pattie different from the others is the ingredients in it and most often, it's fried.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil
  • Cabbage,Onions,tomatoes,bell peppers
  • Seasoning
  • Herring

Steps

    Prepare the dough

  1. Add the flour in a large bowl.
  2. Add the water and oil
  3. Knead the dough until just combined
  4. Roll into a small log
  5. Cut the dough into 4 even pieces.You can make the patties as big or small as youâ€™d like. We cut our dough into 4 pieces.
  6. Working with one piece at a time, flatten the dough. Use a rolling pin if necessary
  7. Add a spoonful or a forkful spoon of the filling to the center of the dough. Fold one half of the dough over to the meat the other half
  8. Press down with your finger firmly, then seal the dough with the back of the fork. Pierce the dough a few time. This will help the dough to cook evenly and allow air to pass through as it cooks.
